About Us

  • We Work In More Than
    10 Countries To
    Strengthen Equality

  • Inequality as we all know has thrived in our Societies for far too long, right from the Colonial era's to even our own new Era we still see some gender favored while the other gender gets neglected that is why our Church ran a Campaign not only in Nigeria but accross the World englightening people on why Inequality should be curbed.

    We started this Campaign years ago and we cannot deny the fact that all of the Countries we've enlightened have taken a major step towards kicking out inequality in the Society at large. We are grateful to God for the success od that Campaign and also the Government of those Countries, They listened to us and did great work.

Who we Are


  • A Church of the Lord

    R.C.C.G Hope Of Glory Parish is a Parish under the Redeemed Christian Church of God Province 52 Area 42. Just like any other Parish in the Reedeemed Christian Church of God. We will be happy to have you in our Church during our weekday Programms or @our Church center in Oke-Aro Agbado, Ogun State. Peace be unto you and your Household. Thank you for visiting our website.


  • Help Is Our Main Goal

    The scripture tells us to give to the needy, even in the Holy Quran we are told to give to the needy as much as we can afford. We the R.C.C.G Hope Of Glory Parish ensure that we give to the Poor, the less priviledged not only gifts, we also equip many youths with skills, we ensure all our Children are in Schools and not on the Road selling things during School Hours. What drives us is the Scripture and not a single person.
